# ClearSight: Dynamic Reading Overlay 👁️✨

**ClearSight** is a professional-grade accessibility tool designed to help individuals with dyslexia and visual processing disorders focus and read more comfortably. It provides a highly customizable "visual anchor" that stays in sync with your reading flow.

## 🚀 Features

- **Motion Themes:** Over 30 specialized patterns including:
  - 🌊 **Liquid Flow:** Ripples, waves, and clouds for gentle anchoring.
  - ⚡ **Neon Beams:** High-contrast, Tron-style "sharp" highlights.
  - 📏 **Reading Rulers:** Horizontal/Vertical masks to eliminate line-crowding.
- **Real-Time Control:** Live sliders for Speed, Opacity, and **Gradient Sharpness**.
- **Always-Available Toggle:** A discrete, movable floating bubble to start/stop the overlay instantly.
- **Smart Autostart:** Automatically triggers when you open reading apps like Kindle or Firefox.

## 🛠️ Installation

1. Download the latest APK from the [Releases](https://github.com/danny1marshall1576/clearsight-overlay/releases) section.
2. Enable **Overlay Permission** and **Accessibility Service** in the app settings.
3. Open your favorite reading app and enjoy a more focused experience.

## 🎨 Customization

ClearSight offers granular control over the "Sharpness" of gradient edges, allowing you to transition from soft, ambient glows to razor-sharp laser lines depending on your visual preference.
